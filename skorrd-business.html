How it works

Simple on both sides

01

List a slot

Post an available appointment time in seconds — service type, time, and your own discount. It goes live immediately for customers nearby.

02

Customer books

Nearby customers find the slot, confirm the details, and pay a small $5 hold fee through Stripe to secure the appointment. No phone calls needed.

03

Both get notified

Instant confirmation for you and the customer. Slot is locked. Customer pays for the service at the appointment — you keep full control of your money.
