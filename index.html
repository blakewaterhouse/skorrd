Policies & pricing

Good to know before you book

1

$5 hold fee

You pay $5 online to secure your slot. This confirms you're serious and protects the business from no-shows. The service fee is paid directly at the appointment.

2

Cancellation policy

You can cancel any time, but the $5 hold fee is non-refundable — it goes to Skorrd. The slot automatically gets re-listed so someone else can grab it.

3

Booking window

Slots are listed up to 7 days ahead with no minimum lead time. Most deals are same-day or next-day — so check back regularly for fresh listings.

4

Secure payments

Hold fees are processed securely via Stripe. Your card details are never stored by Skorrd. The business receives payment directly at the appointment.
